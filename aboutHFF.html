About Hanover Flag Football

Welcome to Hanover Flag Football (HFF) — a fast, fun, inclusive way to play the game. Our league is built for players of all experience levels, with a focus on sportsmanship, skill development, and creating an awesome community atmosphere.

We play on Saturdays at the Hanover Soccer Fields, with age groups from U6 to U18.

Our teams are coached by parent volunteers, with drills and play ideas provided to support you throughout the season. Coaching is a great way to be involved in your child’s team. If you’d like to coach, just let us know when you register or send us a quick email.

What We’re About
  • Fun first: a positive, high-energy environment every week.
  • Skill development: coaches focus on fundamentals and confidence.
  • Fair play: respect for teammates, opponents, and officials.
  • Community: a league built for families, friends, and fans.
How Flag Football Works

Flag football is a non-contact version of football where players “tackle” by pulling a flag from the ball carrier’s belt. It keeps the action fast while emphasizing agility, teamwork, and strategy.
